## 🎯 Strategy

**Lead with Candice's world → reveal product through characters → convert to waitlist/download**

- Phase 1: Establish the world (characters + wardrobes)
- Phase 2: Introduce the mechanism (how the app works)
- Phase 3: Conversion (waitlist / beta / download)

**Platforms:** Instagram (primary), 小红书, TikTok/Douyin
**Formats:** Carousels, slideshow reels, subtle pan videos, before/after transitions
**Voice:** Tender, observant, stylish, lightly magical. Never tech-bro. Never "AI hack."

## 📐 Format Specs

### Carousel
- 1080×1350 (portrait, IG optimal)
- 6-10 slides max
- First slide = hook, last slide = soft CTA
- Consistent color palette per post

### Slideshow Reel
- 1080×1920 (9:16 vertical)
- 15-30 seconds
- Still images with subtle zoom/pan
- Text overlays + music
- No voiceover required

### Before/After Reel
- 1080×1920
- Clean transition (cut, morph, or swipe)
- Real photo → avatar → sticker breakdown
- 10-20 seconds

## 🎨 Brand Guidelines (for content)

- **Colors:** Coral #D0705A, Warm Grey #E5E1DD, Black #111111
- **Font vibe:** Clean, slightly editorial (Bodoni Moda for accent, clean sans for body)
- **Photo style:** Warm, natural light, lifestyle not studio
- **Sticker style:** Loft/Japanese journal aesthetic, white rim, transparent bg
- **Copy tone:** Personal diary, not brand manager. First person when possible.
- **Never say:** "AI-powered," "revolutionary," "game-changing," "hack"
- **Do say:** Names. Feelings. Specific details. The kind of thing a friend would text you.
