How It Works
Three steps to your sticker wardrobe
📸

Upload your OOTDs

Share your outfit photos. Put Together sees every piece — tops, bottoms, shoes, bags, layers — and learns how you wear them together.

🎨

Get your sticker wardrobe

Every garment becomes a Japanese watercolor sticker. Your accessories, your shoes, your coats — all rendered in a style that's yours.

✨

Get dressed by AI

Each morning, Put Together checks the weather, reads your calendar, and picks the perfect outfit from what you own. Your avatar models it for you.
